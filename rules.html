Black-Jack

Objective:

Each player's cards must sum to, or get as close, to 21 without going over.

If a player goes over 21, they lose.

How to play:

Each player will get dealt two cards at the beginning of the round.

The "Hit" button will deal a card to the current players hand.

The "Stay" button will end the players turn.

The player who has 21 or is closer to 21 wins.
